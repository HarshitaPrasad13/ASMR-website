ASMR

Researchers suggest that ASMR may cause the brain to release certain neurohormones. They explain that people report feeling sleepy, comforted, and relaxed with ASMR and that the neurohormones dopamine, oxytocin, and endorphins are closely associated with these feelings.
It has real benefits. ASMR can help enhance mindfulness, reduce your heart rate, and even put you in a focused, flow-like mental state.
